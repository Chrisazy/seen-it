Auto-skip Imgur posts you've already seen

Browse Imgur

Scroll through Imgur how you normally do. Frontpage, usersub, whatever you want. The image you see are saved by their ID.

Skip seen posts

When you land on a post you've already seen, Seen It will skip past the post automatically. Super simple stuff.
