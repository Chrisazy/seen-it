Browse Imgur

Scroll through Imgur how you normally do. Frontpage, usersub, whatever you want. Seen It saves the images you see.

Skip seen posts

When you land on a post you've already seen, Seen It will skip past the post automatically. Super simple stuff.

All the code is open source at github.com/asarode/seen-it. Your pull requests are very welcome!
